Audio Assistance System for Visually Impaired

Globally, an estimated 2.2 billion people experience some form of visual impairment, according to the World Health Organization (WHO). Among them, approximately 36 million are blind, while 217 million have moderate to severe vision impairment. With a mission to make the lives of the 253 million visually impaired independent, Assist is a system which gives voice assistance through Machine Learning.

About

An advanced audio assistance system for blind individuals incorporates multiple features to enhance navigation and interaction. It uses object detection to identify nearby obstacles, providing auditory alerts and distance predictions to avoid collisions. The system includes a GPS-based guidance component, enabling users to receive verbal directions when traveling from one location to another. Additionally, it integrates facial recognition to help identify and announce the presence of known relatives or friends, offering a comprehensive solution for enhanced independence and safety.
